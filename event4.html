ZYVERSE 2K25

SRM Valliammai Engineering College · Sept 13th, 2025

PROMPT PROTOCOL

Where speed meets strategy, and knowledge takes the win! Compete in fast-paced technical rounds that test your wit, knowledge, and teamwork.

Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

• Each team must consist of 2–3 members.
• Teams must register prior to the start of the event.
• The use of mobile phones, internet, or any external resources during the rounds is strictly prohibited.
• Decisions made by the event coordinators/judges will be final and binding.
• Teams must maintain discipline and fair play throughout the event. Misconduct may lead to disqualification.

Round 1 – Tech Feud:

• Teams will be asked a series of technical survey-style questions.
• The goal is to guess the most popular answers (not necessarily the factual one, but the one most people would say).
• Each correct guess scores points based on its popularity.
• Time limit: Each question must be answered within 30 seconds.
• Top-scoring teams will qualify for the next round.

Round 2 – Byte the Clue:

• One member gives a single-word clue, and teammates must guess the correct technical term.
• No gestures, additional words, or spelling out are allowed.
• Each team gets a set of terms to guess within a given time (e.g., 90 seconds).
• Correct guesses earn points; skipping is allowed but with a small penalty.
• The team with the highest cumulative score at the end is declared the winner.

Disqualification Rules:

• Use of unfair means (mobile phones, outside help).
• Giving more than one clue word in Round 2.
• Exceeding time limits repeatedly despite warnings.
• Disruptive behavior.
