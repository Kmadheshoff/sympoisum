CASE 404

Case 404 is a 2-round detective challenge testing logic, observation, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

Round 1: Decode puzzles to qualify for the crime scene.

Round 2: Investigate a live crime scene, analyze clues, and submit a final report.

Rules & Guidelines

1. Teams of 1-2 members.

2. Register before the event.

3. Round 1: Solve 2 puzzles (20 mins), top 5-6 teams qualify.

4. Round 2: Live crime scene investigation (5 mins).

5. Fair play and discipline required.

6. Scoring based on correctness, reasoning, and teamwork.

7. Strict time limits enforced.
