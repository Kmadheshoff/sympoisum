About

ZYVERSE 2K25 marks the third edition of the symposium organized by the Cyber Security Department at SRM Valliammai Engineering College. This event brings together aspiring cybersecurity enthusiasts, students, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements, innovations, and emerging threats in the world of cyber security. Over the years, ZYVERSE has grown into a platform that fosters collaboration, knowledge sharing, and hands-on experience. Participants get an opportunity to engage in workshops, technical sessions, competitions, and panel discussions that cover a wide range of cybersecurity topics—from network security, ethical hacking, and blockchain security, to AI-driven threat detection and secure IoT systems. ZYVERSE 2K25 aims not only to educate but also to inspire participants to develop practical solutions to real-world cybersecurity challenges. By providing exposure to cutting-edge tools and methodologies, the symposium encourages students and professionals alike to innovate, collaborate, and contribute to a safer digital world. Join us on September 13th, 2025, as we continue the journey of empowering the next generation of cybersecurity experts, fostering a community of innovation, and celebrating the spirit of digital resilience.
