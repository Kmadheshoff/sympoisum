CODE IN BORDERLAND

DEADPOOL & WOLVERINE EDITION

A high-energy coding survival challenge where participants step into a gamified arena of logic, programming, and problem solving.

Rules & Guidelines

1) Eligibility:

Open to individual participants or teams (maximum of 2-3 members).

2) Registration:

All participants must register online before the event. Late entries will not be entertained.

3) Fair Play:

Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or external assistance will result in immediate disqualification.

4) Devices & Setup:

Participants must use the systems provided at the venue or the official event website (online). Personal devices may only be used if permitted by organizers.

5) Scoring:

Points are awarded based on correctness, efficiency, and speed. Bonus points may be given for early completion. Wrong answers may lead to loss of "life points".

6) Eliminations:

Certain rounds may have eliminations based on score thresholds. Only top scorers advance.

7) Time Limits:

Each challenge has strict time limits. Submissions after the timer ends will not be accepted.

8) Code of Conduct:

Maintain discipline and follow the instructions of the coordinators. Misbehavior can result in elimination.

9) Organizers' Decision:

The judges' and organizers' decisions are final and binding.
